ABOUT

Our Products




Baked fresh. Baked delicious. Baked by Tiny Cake Bakery

We take pride in using natural ingredients and baking our cupcakes, cakes and desserts from scratch. This landmark bakery is where locals and visitors go when only oven-fresh will do. Since Day One, the commitment has been to keep customers happy with fresh cakes and pastries straight from the ovens just behind the wall. To keep this promise, Tiny Cake Bakery fires up its ovens at 2 a.m., baking around the clock, and they don't quit till 10 p.m., every day. Experienced bakers, some who are 20- or 30-year veterans, use the same time-honored baking methods, making everything from scratch with the freshest island ingredients.

HISTORY




Both of my grandparents used to let me help in the kitchen when my mom was a child, doing anything from peeling carrots to making cinnamon rolls from scratch. Baking was my favorite pastimes with them, and it has continued to be an activity that brings joy to my life. Since I have kids in 1970s, I decided to stay home full time and over the years I have shared my passion for baking with my kids. In 1980, I made the leap and rented some commercial kitchen space to produce small batches of hand-crafted cookies. The response from customers was incredible and our business took off. We have built a loyal following since it opened its doors in 1960. In 2002, my kids, and my grandkids later on, started taken over our Tiny Cake Bakery. They and their kids have opened several more stores in LA County.
